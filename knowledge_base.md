## 3. Menu Details
* **Loaded Potatoes:** A massive russet potato topped with butter, sour cream, shredded cheese, and your choice of Smoked Brisket, Chicken, or Pork.
* **Loaded Fries:** Crinkle-cut fries smothered in white queso and topped with BBQ meat.
* **Loaded Nachos:** Tortilla chips, queso, black beans, corn salsa, and BBQ meat.
* **Loaded Salads:** Fresh greens, cucumbers, tomatoes, and cheese topped with smoked meat.
